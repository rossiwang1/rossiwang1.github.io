About Me 🌟

As a competitive sailor navigating unpredictable winds,

I've learned to turn challenges into momentum.

Each wave teaches resilience, each port brings wisdom,

Now my compass points to Temple's shore.

As a National First-Class Athlete in sailing and an Asian Championship medalist, I've transformed my childhood fear of water into a passion that shapes my future. My journey combines athletic excellence with academic innovation, particularly in sports science and healthcare research.

作为一名帆船项目的国家一级运动员和亚锦赛奖牌获得者，我将童年对水的恐惧转化为塑造未来的动力。我的旅程将运动卓越与学术创新相结合，特别是在运动科学和医疗健康研究方面。

Initiative

  • VR-based sailing simulation system
  • Professional sailing theory courses
  • Regular training workshops

创建天普首个"陆地帆船模拟器"社团，包括：

  • VR帆船模拟系统
  • 专业帆船理论课程
  • 定期培训工作坊

🔬 Sports Medicine Innovator

Purpose

Advance sports injury prevention and rehabilitation through interdisciplinary research

通过跨学科研究推进运动损伤预防和康复

  • Rehabilitation protocol development
